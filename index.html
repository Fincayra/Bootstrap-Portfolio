About Me



My name is Jay and I am 28 years old, living in Lafayette, Colorado with two dogs and my little black cat. Some of my bigger aspirations include web development, traveling abroad, and settling down somewhere to write novels, but in the meantime I enjoy drawing, easy adventures, casual writing, and some light gaming. I come from a family of artists and have a keen eye for design; I've been building web pages for fun since I was in middle school. In 2017 I was diagnosed with brain cancer and have undergone several surgeries and treatments since, but I'm not going to let any of that stop me. I still have big dreams of a successful, code-filled future.
